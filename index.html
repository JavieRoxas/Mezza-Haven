House Rules

👥 Guests & Visitors
1
Maximum of 6 guests for all 3 bedrooms. No unregistered overnight guests.
2
Visitors are allowed 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM only. All visitors must register at the lobby.
3
Celebrations (e.g. birthdays) must be declared in advance for approval. No loud speakers, videoke, confetti, glitter, party poppers, or open flames.
4
Event/cleaning fee of ₱500 may apply if the unit is left excessively dirty.
⛔ Not Allowed
Parties, loud music, subletting, commercial shoots, or any disruptive activity that may disturb neighbors or violate SMDC / HOA rules. Violation may result in immediate termination of stay without refund.
🔇 Noise & Conduct
Quiet hours are strictly from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Please keep noise to a minimum and respect staff and neighbors at all times. Violations may lead to penalties.
🚬 Strict No-Smoking / No-Vaping Policy
No smoking or vaping anywhere inside the unit or on balconies.
Violation fee: ₱2,000 minimum + additional charges for damages/odor removal
🐾 No Pets
Pets are not allowed inside the unit.
🧹 Cleanliness, Care & Damages
1
CLAYGO — Clean As You Go. Dispose of trash in designated areas only.
2
Close all windows and sliding doors during rain or wind to prevent water damage.
3
No writing or drawing on walls.
4
No belongings left outside the unit door (e.g. shoes, mats). Building staff may remove unattended items.
5
Report damages or malfunctions to the host immediately.
💸 Typical Non-Compliance Fees
Extra cleaning: ₱700 (beyond standard turnover)
Wall cleaning/repainting: from ₱2,000
💡 Utilities & Appliances
Turn off A/C, lights, and all appliances when leaving the unit. Do not tamper with electrical systems, WiFi, or building systems.
🏢 Condo & HOA Compliance
Follow all SMDC Mezza Residences condominium policies. Violations (e.g. unregistered guests, smoking, parties) may incur fines or eviction without refund.
